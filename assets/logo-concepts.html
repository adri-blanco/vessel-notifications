AIS Notify

Six design directions for a maritime AIS vessel notification system running on Raspberry Pi. Each explores a different visual metaphor from the product's core ideas.

01
Radar Sweep
AIS range arcs fan from a fixed station. Vessel echoes appear at distance — the exact moment of reception made visible.
02
Ship Signal
Vessel profile with AIS arcs broadcasting to the right. The most literal read of the product — a ship transmitting its identity.
03
Anchor Tech
The anchor's ring replaced by an AIS signal arc. Maritime heritage and radio technology merged into a single symbol.
04
Track Grid
Vessel positions fade in across a coordinate grid, growing brighter toward the latest sighting. Pure data, pure sea.
05
Signal Bell
Notification bell with AIS arcs rising above it. The whole product promise in one icon — a vessel is heard, you are told.
06
Crosshair Lock
A targeting reticle locked onto a vessel silhouette. Precision surveillance — every AIS transmission located and recorded.
